# HCC Insurance Holdings (hcc-insurance-holdings)

HCC Insurance Holdings was a leading specialty insurance group with offices in
the United States, the United Kingdom, Spain, and Ireland. It was acquired by
Tokio Marine in 2015 and now operates as Tokio Marine HCC, providing more
than 100 classes of specialty insurance products in approximately 180
countries.
